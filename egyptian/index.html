Hieroglyph editor

Simple editor for previewing text from the Egyptian Hieroglyphs, Egyptian Hieroglyphs Extended-A, and Egyptian Hieroglyph Format Controls Unicode blocks.

Shortcuts for hieroglyphs:

Hex value: \u13xxx,

Gardiner code: \g_(Gardiner code),

\u_13000, or \g_a1, outputs to 𓀀.

Shortcuts for uniliterals:

Standard MdC, plus uppercase variants for w, y, m, n, and g.

\u_1340d, or \g_aa1, or x outputs to 𓐍.
